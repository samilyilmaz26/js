Access the app from your mobile
Both your computer and mobile should be on the same Wi-Fi network.
Find your computer’s local IP address (e.g., 192.168.1.5)
On your mobile browser, open:
http://192.168.1.5:5173
You may need to allow your Vite dev server to listen on the network. You can run:
vite --host
